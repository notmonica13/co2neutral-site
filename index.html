Our Services

Cost Engineering & Design‑to‑Cost

Optimise product and process costs from design through production. We build should‑cost models and use design‑to‑cost methodologies to meet your target costs.

CO₂ Footprint Analysis

Gain a clear picture of the carbon emissions of your products and processes. We model different scenarios and recommend actions to reduce emissions.

Lifecycle Costing

Evaluate total cost of ownership across the entire product life cycle – from materials and manufacturing to end‑of‑life – to inform strategic decisions.

Make‑or‑Buy & Negotiation Support

Decide whether to manufacture components in‑house or source them externally. We verify supplier cost claims and support fact‑based negotiations.

Cost & Carbon Cleansheeting

Integrated cleansheet models combine bottom‑up cost and CO₂ analysis, providing transparency for sustainable decision‑making.

Industries We Serve

We specialise in automotive and industrial manufacturing sectors. Whether you are developing electric vehicles, improving traditional powertrains or optimising supply chains, we provide tailored cost and CO₂ consulting solutions.
